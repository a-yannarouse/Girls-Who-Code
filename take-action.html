Autistic people are different but the same as us. They didn't ask to have autism and it is not a bad thing but the world thinks differently. They deserve their natural rights just like any other human. I encourage you to be more involved and aware of the autism community because of misconceptions, the autism community is mistreated. This is important because the more the people understand and accept autistic people, they can live life without any discrimination regarding applying to jobs, homes, and social life. With your help, we can make for the autism community and make sure they have the same equal rights as everyone else. Autism is not a mental illness and it never will be.
